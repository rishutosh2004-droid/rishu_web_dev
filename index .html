About Us

Tezpur School, established in 1985, is a premier institution in Assam known for its commitment to academic excellence and all-round student development. With highly qualified faculty, modern classrooms, and a vibrant campus, we provide a nurturing environment where every child’s potential is realized.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower students with knowledge, values, and skills to thrive in a global society while staying rooted in cultural heritage.

Mission: To nurture young minds through innovative teaching, extracurricular engagement, and a strong emphasis on discipline and creativity.

Principal’s Message

“At Tezpur School, we believe education goes beyond textbooks. Our goal is to create confident, compassionate, and responsible citizens. With a blend of academics, sports, and cultural activities, we strive to prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.”

Our Facilities

Modern Classrooms

Smart boards, audio-visual tools, and interactive learning methods for an engaging classroom experience.

Library

A vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to encourage reading and research.

Science Labs

Well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to promote hands-on learning.

Sports Complex

Playgrounds, indoor stadium, and dedicated coaches for various sports and physical activities.

Student Activities

Our students actively participate in various activities that promote teamwork, leadership, and creativity:

  • Sports: Football, Cricket, Badminton, Athletics, and more.
  • Cultural: Annual Day, Music, Dance, Drama, and Art Competitions.
  • Clubs: Science Club, Eco Club, Debate Society, and Literary Circle.
